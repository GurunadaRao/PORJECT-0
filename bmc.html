Business Model Canvas

Understanding the business aspects and market strategy of our smart home automation solution

Key Partners

  • 1. Hardware Component Manufacturers – Provide essential electronic parts
  • 2. IoT & Software Developers – Build app and alert systems
  • 3. Safety Certification Bodies – Approve product safety
  • 4. Marketing & Sales Partners – Promote and sell the product
  • 5. Government Bodies – Set safety regulations

Key Activities

  • IoT software development
  • Product development
  • Testing
  • Installation and maintenance
  • Data analysis
  • Marketing

Value Propositions

  • Real-Time Gas Leak Detection
  • Automatic Preventive Actions
  • Immediate Alerts and Notifications
  • Cost-Effective and Scalable
  • Enhanced Monitoring and Control
  • Advanced Kitchen Safety

Customer Relationships

  • B2B:
    • Dedicated support and maintenance contracts for property managers
    • Staff training on system use
  • B2C:
    • Real-time alerts and self-service monitoring via app
    • Resident feedback through the app

Customer Segments

  • 1. RESIDENTS
    • Especially urban households using LPG
  • 2. GATED COMMUNITIES
    • Families with children or elderly
  • 3. Restaurants
    • Hotels, canteens, food delivery kitchens
  • 4. Industrial Clients / Small Factories
    • Warehouses storing flammable substances

Key Resources

  • 1. Engineering Team
  • 2. Hardware Components
  • 3. Cloud Platform
  • 4. App
  • 5. Certifications

Channels

  • 1. Awareness
    • Digital marketing (social media, SEO, Google Ads)
    • Influencer reviews / YouTube demos
    • Tech expos or smart home fairs
  • 2. Product website with demo videos and FAQs
  • 3. Product trials or demos in stores
  • 4. Smart home solution providers or consultants
  • 5. Delivery
  • 6. Mobile app for real-time monitoring
  • 7. Annual Maintenance Contract

Cost Structure

  • Component/Expense Approx. Cost per Unit (INR)
  • Hardware components (ESP32, sensor, servo, etc.) ₹1,200 – ₹1,800
  • Assembly & Testing ₹150 – ₹300
  • Enclosure & Finishing ₹150 – ₹300
  • Packaging & Labeling ₹100 – ₹200
  • Marketing/Branding per unit ₹100 – ₹200
  • Miscellaneous (Transport, buffer, etc.) ₹100 – ₹150

Revenue Streams

  • 1. Hardware Sales: One-time purchase of devices
  • 2. Annual maintenance contracts: Subscriptions for annual maintenance of product
  • 3. Installation: Optional service for installing devices
  • 4. Institutional Sales: Collaborating with Apartments, Ware Houses storing Flammable gases(LPG)
